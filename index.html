SOC 201: World Geography

Globalization and Cultural Diffusion

Cultural diffusion is the spread of cultural beliefs and social activities from one group to another. The mixing of world cultures through different ethnicities, religions, and nationalities has increased with advanced communication.

1. Economic Interdependence

In the modern era, no nation exists in isolation. Economic interdependence refers to the relationship between two or more countries that rely on each other for necessary goods.
